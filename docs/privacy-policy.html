Dhakira Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Dhakira ("the App") is a note-taking and task management app for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Access

Google Calendar: If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, the App requests permission to create, update, and delete calendar events on your behalf. We access only the calendar events scope and your email address to identify your account.

Apple Calendar: If enabled, the App creates events in your local Apple Calendar using EventKit. This data stays on your device and iCloud.

Data We Store

All your notes, tasks, and app data are stored locally on your device and synced via Apple iCloud (CloudKit). We do not operate any servers that store your personal data.

Google OAuth tokens are stored securely in your device's Keychain. We do not transmit or store these tokens on any external server.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information, analytics, usage data, or advertising identifiers. There are no third-party trackers, SDKs, or analytics services in the App.

Third-Party Services

The App connects to Google Calendar API solely to sync your task events. No other third-party services receive your data.

Data Deletion

You can disconnect your Google Calendar at any time from the App's settings. This removes all stored tokens from your device. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
